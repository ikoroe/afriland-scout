# Demo Video Script — Afriland Scout

**Total time:** ~3:30  
**Equipment:** Your phone (screen recording + front/rear camera)  
**Location:** Any open land or compound you can walk around  

---

## 0:00–0:20 — Personal Hook (selfie camera, you talking)

> "A few years ago, I tried to buy land in Umuahia, Nigeria.
> Before paying, I had no way to verify the actual size of the land
> without hiring an expensive surveyor.
>
> This is a common problem across Africa — land buying is opaque,
> manual, and prone to fraud.
>
> So I built Afriland Scout."

---

## 0:20–0:35 — What It Does (switch to screen recording of the app)

> "Afriland Scout is an AI land surveyor that runs on your phone.
> You walk the land, and it captures boundaries, calculates area,
> and tells you exactly what you're buying — all in real time."

*Show the app dashboard. Tap Connect. Let the agent greet you briefly (5 seconds max).*

---

## 0:35–1:40 — The Core Demo (walk and record points)

> "Let me show you. I'm standing at the first corner of this plot."

*Tap 📍 Record GPS Point. Show the coordinate chip appear.*

> "Point one captured."

*Walk to the second corner.*

> "Second corner."

*Tap 📍 Record GPS Point.*

*Walk to third corner, tap again. Walk to fourth corner, tap again.*

> "Four boundary points recorded. Now let's see the result."

*Tap 📐 Calculate Area.*

> "The agent calculated the land area using geodesic measurement —
> that's the same math professional surveyors use.
> It shows me the size in square meters and in Nigerian plots."

*Tap 🗺️ Show Map.*

> "And here's the plot boundary on a satellite map."

*Let the map image load and display for a few seconds.*

---

## 1:40–2:20 — Voice Interaction (start mic, talk to agent)

> "But Afriland Scout isn't just buttons. It's a voice agent you can talk to."

*Tap Start mic.*

*Say to the agent:* "How many plots of 600 square meters fit on this land?"

*Let the agent respond with audio. Pause and let it speak.*

*Say:* "Is this a good size for a 3-bedroom house?"

*Let the agent respond.*

---

## 2:20–2:50 — Vision Demo (start camera, show the land)

> "The agent can also see through my camera."

*Tap Start camera. Pan your phone across the land slowly.*

*Say to the agent:* "What do you see? Describe the terrain."

*Let the agent respond based on what the camera is showing.*

---

## 2:50–3:10 — Tech Stack (quick, keep walking or standing)

> "Under the hood, this is powered by Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash
> through the Live API — real-time voice and vision over WebSocket.
>
> The backend runs on FastAPI, deployed on Google Cloud Run.
> GPS coordinates are computed using geodesic math on the WGS84 ellipsoid.
> And the plot boundary renders on Google Maps."

---

## 3:10–3:30 — Closing (back to selfie camera)

> "Afriland Scout turns any smartphone into a land intelligence tool.
>
> No expensive surveyor. No guesswork.
> Just walk, talk, and know what you're buying.
>
> Built for the Gemini Live Agent Challenge."

*End.*

---

## Tips for Recording

- **Screen record the app portions** using your phone's built-in screen recorder
- **Use selfie camera** for the intro and closing
- **Use rear camera** when showing the land for the vision demo
- **Speak clearly and naturally** — you're a builder showing your work, not reading a script
- **Don't worry about perfection** — authenticity beats polish at hackathons
- **Keep it under 4 minutes** — judges appreciate conciseness
